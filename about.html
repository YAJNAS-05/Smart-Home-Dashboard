Our Story

ZoneWise was founded in 2025 by a team of smart home enthusiasts who were frustrated with the limitations of existing home automation systems. We noticed that most solutions treated homes as single units rather than collections of distinct spaces with different needs.

Our breakthrough came when we developed the first truly zone-aware smart home controller, allowing different areas of a home to operate independently while still working together as a cohesive system. Today, ZoneWise powers thousands of homes across the country, helping families live more comfortably while saving energy.

What started as a passion project in a garage has grown into a leading smart home technology company, but we've never lost sight of our original vision: to create intuitive, powerful home automation that respects the unique character of every space in your home.
